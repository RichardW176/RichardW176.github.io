**10: EXT. PUBLIC SQUARE - MORNING**

*Morning, in the same square. DIEGO is sitting upright, shaking his leg, looking at his phone. People pass left and right, but not the person he's waiting for. Until there she is. ANNIE, wearing her Pepperdine sweater. DIEGO's eyes light up and he stands.*

**ANNIE**
What do you want with me, Diego?

**DIEGO**
I just wanted to talk.

**ANNIE**
Then talk.

**DIEGO**
What I asked that day, I didn't think it through. I'm sorry.

**ANNIE**
Its ok.

*ANNIE sits down. DIEGO sits down next to her.*

**DIEGO**
I just want things to go back to the way they were.

**ANNIE**
Why are you even with me, Diego?

**DIEGO**
Because I really like you.

**ANNIE**
Do you like me or the idea of dating me?

**DIEGO**
How- how can you even ask that?

**ANNIE**
Because I don't know what you want Diego- you don't even know what you want! You come home every night from a different station working a different job and nobody knows where you've gone- not me, not your parents.

**DIEGO**
Because I'm scared, Annie.

**ANNIE**
Scared of what?

**DIEGO**
Everything!

*DIEGO's exclamation turns a few heads. He lowers his tone.*

**DIEGO (CONT'D)**
My parents used to say that I could go anywhere and do anything I wanted when I grew up. Next thing I know, they're telling me to go somewhere across the country for four years. Then they'll stop asking me to come home. And suddenly I'll have a place of my own, working the same lousy job every day. And it'll never be the same-

**ANNIE**
But that's life Diego.

**DIEGO**
Then I don't want anything to do with it.

**ANNIE**
You can't keep walking away from responsibility.

**DIEGO**
Isn't that what you wanted?

**ANNIE**
But right now all I am is your way of moving on, Diego, of you stabilizing your life. And I can't do that right now.

**DIEGO**
Why not?

**ANNIE**
Because I have my whole life ahead of me. And you do too. I can't be your world, Diego.

**DIEGO**
And yet you are.

*ANNIE takes out a cigarette and struggles to light it. She gives up and lets out a deep sigh.*

**ANNIE**
I think I'm gonna go.

**DIEGO**
Where are you going?

**ANNIE**
I don't know. Back to university. Back to Cat Town. I don't know and I don't care. Anywhere but here.

**DIEGO**
What about Your Cat?

**ANNIE**
You can keep her.

*ANNIE begins to leave.*

**DIEGO**
Annie?

*ANNIE stops.*

**ANNIE**
Don't come home, Diego.

*ANNIE leaves. DIEGO is left alone in the plaza, looking off into the distance.*
